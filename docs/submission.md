# Submission

## How to Submit
Submission site: **TBD** (we will post the official submission link here)

## Format and Anonymity
- File format: **PDF only**
- Template: **A4 IEEE conference template**
- Review style: **single-blind**, so author names and affiliations should appear in the paper
- Include keywords in the manuscript

## Track Selection
When submitting, select one of:
- Full paper
- Short or position paper
- Poster

## Originality
Submissions must be original work and not simultaneously under review elsewhere.

## Camera-Ready
Camera-ready instructions and the final deadline will be shared with acceptance notifications. Authors of accepted papers will be asked to incorporate reviewer feedback and comply with the final formatting requirements.

## Presentation and Attendance
At least one author of each accepted contribution should register for FLICS 2026 and present the work at the workshop.
